## ğŸ¯ Strategy Tips

### Training Phase (Days 1-4)

**Balanced Approach:**
- Train each attribute 3 times
- Creates well-rounded fighter
- Good for beginners

**Specialist Approach:**
- Focus on 1-2 attributes
- Max out Striking or Grappling
- Higher risk, higher reward

### Fighter Selection

**Research Before Transfer:**
- Check fighter's recent record
- Look at upcoming events
- Consider their division strength
- Check their ranking

**Best Practices:**
- Choose active fighters (fighting soon)
- Pick fighters with winning streaks
- Avoid injury-prone fighters
- Consider championship fights (more events)

### Ladder Climbing

**Preliminary Card Strategy:**
- Need 5 wins - be patient
- Low coin value but fast fights
- Build confidence and rhythm
- Losses don't reset progress

**Main Card Strategy:**
- 3 wins needed - mid-game
- Better coin value (3/win)
- Pick fighters carefully
- Balance wins vs speed

**Champion Strategy:**
- 5 total wins to retire
- Maximum coins (5/win)
- Choose only the best
- Think long-term

### Retirement Planning

**When to Retire:**
- After 5 champion wins (automatic)
- You'll see retirement notice
- Review your total coin earnings

**After Retirement:**
- Keep all Fan Coins
- Start fresh Rookie
- Try different weight class
- Build different stat specialization
- Climb leaderboard further!
