**Overall: ~5.7/10** — a fun *destination* once you're in a game, but little pulling users *back* the next day. The highest-leverage work is retention, not new features.

## Feature-by-feature notes

- **Home** — Best asset: the next-event countdown creates urgency. Make it the hub for a daily reward + missions (below).
- **Slots / Poker** — Most "fun per second" in the app. Polished feedback loops. Keep investing here; these are your hook for the competitive-gamer persona.
- **Train to UFC** — Already has the right retention DNA (energy that refills daily, streaks, leaderboard). It's the only feature actively asking users to come back — generalize that pattern app-wide.
- **Predictions / Fantasy** — Natural fit for the hardcore-bettor persona and for fight-week spikes, but not currently wrapped in streaks/rewards, so there's no reason to predict *every* event.
- **Forums / Live Chat** — Community is present but disconnected from the coin economy; no incentive to post. Casual fans have little reason to engage.
- **Road to UFC / mini-games** — Good content depth; risk is discoverability and a clear progression goal.

## Top 5 improvements (prioritized)

1. **Global daily-login streak + reward — effort S, impact High.**
   One escalating reward for consecutive days (Day 1: 50 coins → Day 7: 500 + bonus), with a streak counter in the header. This is the single biggest return-rate lever and the app already has the coin plumbing. *Problem it solves: "Reasons to return" = 4/10.*

2. **Daily missions board — effort M, impact High.**
   3 rotating daily tasks that span features ("make 1 fight prediction", "spin slots 5×", "post in a forum") rewarding coins. Forces discovery of the whole app and gives casual users a reason to touch predictions + community. *Solves the "scattered 5 games" friction.*

3. **Fight-week re-engagement (web push + email) — effort M, impact High.**
   UFC's calendar is your built-in retention engine. Notify "UFC 3XX is tomorrow — lock your predictions & earn coins." Pair with a temporary **Fight-Week Event** mode (boosted rewards). Note: no push/messaging is wired up yet (Coder Agent can add Firebase Cloud Messaging / web-push). *Captures the natural traffic spike the Market Research agent will quantify.*

4. **Unified seasonal leaderboard + a coin sink — effort M/L, impact Med-High.**
   One global, resetting (monthly "season") leaderboard, plus something meaningful to *spend* coins on — cosmetics, fighter card packs, profile flair. Gives earning a purpose and adds bragging rights. **Depends on Coder audit #1** (server-authoritative coins) or the leaderboard is meaningless.

5. **First-session onboarding — effort S/M, impact Med.**
   A 3-step guided tour ending in an instant coin grant + the user's first prediction, converting one-time visitors (esp. from the SEO/Social agents' traffic) into signed-up returnees. *Raises First-session hook + signup conversion.*
